We live in the Marigny, but wedding events are all over the place, so don't feel obligated. If you do stay around here, there's plenty of nice strolls to have, cafes to sit at, and interesting things to eat. We're fans of AirBNB, but you may not be, and that's cool too, but it may be harder to find a hotel.
